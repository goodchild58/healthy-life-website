Healthy Eating

Fruits and vegetables are vital for providing essential nutrients. A balanced diet should include:

  • Fresh fruits like apples, bananas, and berries.
  • Vegetables such as spinach, broccoli, and carrots.
  • Whole grains and lean proteins.

Example Diet Plan:

  • Breakfast: Oatmeal with fruits.
  • Lunch: Grilled chicken with quinoa and steamed veggies.
  • Dinner: Salmon with a side of salad.

Exercise for Wellness

Regular exercise boosts energy, improves mood, and promotes overall health. Recommended activities:

  • 30 minutes of brisk walking daily.
  • Strength training twice a week.
  • Yoga or meditation for mental clarity.
